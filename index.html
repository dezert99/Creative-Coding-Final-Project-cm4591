Instructions
  • W to move in the direction the ship is facing
  • S to stop movement
  • E to shoot
  • Move mouse to change direction
  • After you shoot, you will need to find a new bullet on the map!
    look out for a white square!
Tips:
  • Asteroids do have mass and will pull you towards them
  • When you shoot your bullet, you need to find another
  • Bullets hurt, don't get hit
